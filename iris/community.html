    Forums

    Two forums have been created in Google groups. The first is for general discussion and asking questions on how to use Iris. The second is for people interested in the development of or in contributing to the library.

    The forums are publicly visible and you do not need a Google account to read or search the posts.

    To take part in one or both of the forums please use your Google account or sign up for one and then request to join following the links below.
